Help Resources

We are here to educate and guide those residents of the City of Chicago who have been ticketed about the appeal process step-by-step. We are here to provide you with a step-to-step guide, and fill-in-the-blank template and useful links to contest your ticket. The average resident does not require the knowledge of a lawyer to present an argument at court. Let us guide you through the contest procedure so that you can advocate for yourself in court and win your case!
