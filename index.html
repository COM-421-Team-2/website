Who Are We?

We are a volunteer-based nonprofit organization founded in December 2020. Our mission is to guide the residents of the 30th ward on how to contest their tickets. We aim to educate those in the community with data and help resources about how to to prepare for a successful court case.

Why Are We Here?

We are on a mission to help residents of the 30th ward to contest and win appeals on parking violations in the City of Chicago.
